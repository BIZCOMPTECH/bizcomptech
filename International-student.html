Study In Singapore

‘Small but mighty’ is an apt description for Singapore. This little red dot might be densely populated, and it might have a small population but the universities here definitely have a global presence as prominent as some of its bigger counterparts like United States. For example, National University of Singapore comes in 24th and is the only Asian university to make it in the top 25 list of the World University Rankings 2016-2017.

Apart from offering a world-class education system for higher education, Singapore can also provide international students with a safe environment. The crime rate in Singapore is low, and students will have a homely feeling in this multi-cultural, multi-racial cosmopolitan. Frequently being referred to as a melting pot of cultures, Singapore can definitely provide a unique worldview for her visiting international students.
